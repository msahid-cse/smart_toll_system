Smart Toll System - Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Smart Toll System.

1. Personal Information We Collect

When you use the Smart Toll System, we collect certain information about you, including:

  • Personal and contact information such as name, email address, and phone number.
  • Vehicle information including license plate number.
  • Payment information for processing toll fees.
  • Usage data such as IP address, browser type, and device information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Smart Toll System.
  • Process toll payments and manage user accounts.
  • Communicate with you regarding your account and system updates.
  • Improve and personalize the user experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes.

3. Data Sharing

We may share your personal information with:

  • Service providers and third-party partners who assist us in operating the Smart Toll System.
  • Law enforcement agencies or regulatory authorities when required by law.
  • Other users in accordance with your preferences and settings.

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your personal information and prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

5. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy and comply with legal obligations.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Withdraw consent for processing your information.
  • Request a copy of your data or transfer it to another service provider.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@smarttollsystem.com.
